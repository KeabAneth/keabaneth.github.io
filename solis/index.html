What is Solis?

lis is a unique weather app that allows you to track and explore weather conditions not only on Earth but also on different planets in our solar system. With an intuitive interface, you can easily view current weather updates, forecasts, and fascinating atmospheric data from various celestial bodies. Whether you're planning your day or dreaming of interplanetary adventures, Solis brings the universe's weather to your fingertips!
